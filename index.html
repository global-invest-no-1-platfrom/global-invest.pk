Privacy Policy

Platform Terms & Conditions

  1. Account Registration: Each user is permitted only one registered account per IP address. Dual registrations will trigger an automated bot suspension.
  2. Information Confidentiality: All personal data, including email addresses, bound phone numbers, and transactional records, are fully encrypted.
  3. Mobile Verification Binds: Phone numbers must be verified and bound to the profile page to enable withdraw features. Bound numbers cannot be altered.
  4. Anti-Bot Mechanism: The platform utilizes dynamic monitoring to block auto-clickers, automated scripts, and external browser extensions.
  5. Deposit Approvals: Deposits must be backed by accurate payment proof (Trx ID & exact receipt details) to successfully activate any VIP package.
  6. Third-Party Payments: Sending funds from or receiving withdrawals to third-party bank accounts not belonging to the registered user is prohibited.
  7. Withdrawal Limits: Withdrawal requests are bound by local limits (minimum Rs. 50, maximum Rs. 50,000 per transaction).
  8. Active Referral Rule: To sustain the community economy, users must satisfy the minimum active team invite condition before making withdrawals.
  9. Earning Discontinuation: The company reserves the right to temporarily pause global advertisement earnings for urgent system maintenance.
  10. Package Integrity: Once an investment VIP package is approved, it remains active for exactly 55 calendar days and cannot be refunded.
  11. Abuse and Defamation: Harassment of staff, customer support agents, or spreading false panic in public groups will result in an immediate permanent ban.
  12. Promotional Abuse: Exploiting promotional codes, promo systems, or register bonuses via fake referrals will result in wallet balance confiscation.
  13. Dynamic Balance Audits: The system periodically audits wallet balances against watched ad histories to detect and eliminate database manipulation.
  14. Service Updates: Terms, deposit methods, commission rates, and notification rules are subject to change without prior personal notification.
  15. Permanent Termination: Persistent violation of any security or economic rule listed herein grants the company immediate authority to terminate the account.
  16. Database Integrity: Any structural attempt to bypass browser restrictions or tamper with local database variables will result in automatic network blocking.
  17. Referral Integrity: Artificially creating sub-accounts under one's own referral code using virtual private networks (VPNs) is strictly forbidden.
  18. Transaction Veracity: All transaction references (Trx ID) are automatically cross-checked with the bank gateway database. Fake submissions lead to immediate account lock.
  19. Server Access Control: Bypassing standard navigation routes or directly calling admin endpoints will trigger server-side IP bans.
  20. Refund Disclaimers: Users agree that digital packages activated through verified deposits are digital services and not subject to cash refunds.
  21. Deposit Resolution: All payout disputes must be resolved directly through official support. Taking disputes to public forums violates terms.
  22. Inactivity Policy: Accounts remaining inactive (no logins or ad views) for more than 90 consecutive days may have their free bonuses recycled.
  23. Commission Auditing: Commission rewards generated from referral deposits are audited manually before withdrawal approval to verify user authenticity.
  24. Dynamic Rate Calibration: System profitability margins and daily ad profits may be adjusted periodically to preserve platform longevity.
